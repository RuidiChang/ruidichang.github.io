About Me

I’m a second-year CS Ph.D. student at Rice University, advised by Prof. Hanjie Chen. I’m interested in interpretable machine learning—especially understanding how language models behave and process information to make decisions. Before Rice, I earned my master’s at Carnegie Mellon University.
